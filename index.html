Story

"You chase Prometheus to his lair to find a machine full of floating comic panels. Prometheus explains that he is a Keyman, a race that lives in between the real and Jump worlds. Their role is to deliver the stories of Jump to humans in the form of comics, to inspire us to live better lives."

Mulitplayer

In Mulitplayer you either choose to play online with alot of different people around the world, or you can choose to do offline and play with your friends or practice your combos to be the best fighter
